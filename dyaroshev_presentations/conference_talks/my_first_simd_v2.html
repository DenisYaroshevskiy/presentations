What's SIMD and how to get it?

Vector Processor Extensions

  • x86
    • 128 bits: SSE2, SSE3, SSSE3, SSE4.1, SSE4.2
    • 256 bits: AVX, AVX2, XOP
    • 512 bits: AVX512 and its myriad of sub-genre
  • ARM
    • 128 bits: NEON, ASIMD
    • SVE (VLS/VLA)
  • PowerPC
  • WASM
